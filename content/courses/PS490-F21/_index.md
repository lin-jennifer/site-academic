## Overview and Goals

The goals of this module is to introduce students to the `ggplot2` package in R. Through three weeks of instruction, students will learn how to build graphs from raw data and enhance the graphs to make them ready for publication. In the first week, students learn how to construct a basic graph, which they will add colors and enhance the labels in the second week and change font sizes and layouts in the third week. Overall, this module is an introduction for advanced beginners in ways to make excellent graphics using R code.
